The pioneer in bagging of bulk materials. Chronos Richardson has developed into the leading supplier of weighing and bagging technology.
 
Integration, configuration, system compatibility - weighing technology of Chronos Richardson
Today's weighing technology of Chronos Richardson is designed to meet the demand for system integration, configuration and compatibility. The systems are easily adjustable to given conditions and requirements. Additionally, our systems can communicate with other data processing systems and can be easily integrated into the production process. In most areas of agriculture and industry the use of weighing systems for automation of production processes is necessary.
 
Precise weight registration flexible and modular
The latest generation of Chronos Richardson automatic weighers is appropriate for verification and stands out due to its high accuracy and performance, flexibility, modularity as well as its convenient operation. Weight indication by the integrated load cell technology follows our trend of continuous improvement.
 
Proven reliability-net weighing with the CHRONO-WEIGH
E-series
When the net weighing method is employed material is fed into a weigh hopper. After the required weight has been reached the weigh hopper is discharged.
The reliable product line of the CHRONO-WEIGH E-series net weighers, proven in thousands of installations, is subject to a continuous optimization process and, therefore, is suited to changing market requirements. Usually, the E-series is used for weighing upto 50 kg, if required, a maximum of 100 kg can be weighed. It is designed to satisfy the most stringent international regulatory requirements to meet the highest demands for accuracy and precision weighing for all bagging needs. The E-series is used in the food and animal feed sector as well as in chemical and construction industries.
Another feature of this family of weighers is the enclosed design with removable doors for easy access to essential parts. This also facilitates cleaning of the machine.
 
Heavy-duty operating conditions
To meet the individual needs and the sometimes challenging requirements of industry our E-series net weighers are available in a number of different variations.
  • Where corrosive materials are handled all material contacted parts or even the entire wiegher can be provided in stainless steel (e.g. fertilizer specification).
  • Where abrasive materials are handled all material contacted parts can be equipped with an anti-wear coating.
  • In hazardous areas the machines can be equipped with components that correspond to the respective classification.
  • Where dusty products are handled or where special hygienic conditions have to be considered, e.g. the handling of milk powder, we can provide special types of hopper and discharge gate as well.
 
Diverse applications
The net weighers of the E-series are designed to suit the following applications:
  • Stand-alone weigher
  • As part of a bagging station in combination with a bag clamp and bag closer
  • Integrated into automatic bagging lines with automatic bag placer, closing device and palletizer for handling of single or multi-ply open mouth.
