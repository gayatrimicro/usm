Open Mouth -  Feeders & Accessories

The method of material feeding determines the success of the weighing process. To determine the most suitable feeding device precise information on flow properties and material characteristics are necessary.
Chronos Richardson has an extensive application knowledge - thousands of bulk materials have been tested and evaluated. Based on this application knowledge Chronos Richardson has developed a wide range of feeding devices for the H, G and E - Scales (Series). which are suitable for a variety of material characteristics.
We will select the feeding system appropriate for careful material handling considerations such as speed, accuracy, material degradation and cleanout. Chronos Richardson offers the following standard feeding systems:
  • Dosing cone Type "KD"
  • Belt feeder Type "B"
  • Gravity feeder Type "G"
  • Vibratory chute feeder Type "V"
  • Gravity feeder with agitator Type "A"
  • Screw feeder Type "SS", "DS" and "RS"
 
The dosing cone is used for fragile, free-flowing or fluidizing materials where it is necessary to maintain the materials ' structure: such as the fine grains of powdery animal feeds and vitamin additives . The filling cross section can be adjusted to full feed and dribble feed by lowering the cone. The dosing cone is made of sintered, air permeable material and during filling operation air is injected into the cone to facilitate a continuous material flow.

The gravity feeder "G" is used of continuous feeding of free-flowing materials, such as plastic granules, granuled fertilizer, grain, rice, sugar, etc. The quantity fed during full feed and dribble feed modes depend on the flow characteristics of the material and the distance the adjustable catch gate is opened. Dosing cross sections and quantities are adjustable.
The "A"-feeder is equal to the "G" type feeder but additionally equipped with an agitator. The "A"-feeder feeds material which does not flow freely. Typical materials in this low performance range are pellets, bran and animal feed. The agitator is motor driven.
 
The belt feeder is used for non-free flowing materials which need to be handled carefully to maintain their structure, such as animal feed or pellets. The belt feeder can be used with a wide range of materials on a medium performance level and produces extraordinarily precise accuracies.
The quantity of the fed material depends on the speed of the conveyor and the feeder cross section opened by the stream depth regulator.
 

The vibratory chute is preferably used for difficult materials which need careful handling, Normally, these materials are not free-flowing , powdery or coarse ground, flaky or pelleted, such as forage flakes, reisn and Caprolactam pellets, coal, coke or minerals, to name only a few.
Type "V" can be cleaned easily and can be adapted to the respective material by additional coatings. The material is fed in full feed mode and in dribble feed mode.
 
To continuously feed non-free flowing, mealy or powdery materials we offer different types of screw feeders - depending on the desired capacity as single or double screw. Bulk materials to be handled, e.g. flours, feed pre-mixes, milk powders, minerals. To meet the demand for purity and hygiene of the bagged material easy access for thorough cleaning is an important feature, e.g. by integration of cleaning doors. reversible configuration of the screws, outlet valves for removal of residues, options for wet cleaning, etc.
If requested, we provide multiple screw feeders for high performance operations Type "RS" which can feed up to 10 surge hoppers for integration into a single weigher. This forms a typical application of the milling industry.
