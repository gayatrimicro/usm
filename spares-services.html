Spares and Service

Union Special Medway leadership in the industrial Weighing, Bag Closing , Industrial Sewing and Process control industries covers not only the supply of the widest range of weighing and process equipment, but also includes service, installation and commissioning. This ensures that all equipment is installed correctly to maintain its design performance for the future.


Complementary to this facility every Union Special Medway customer enjoys a fast delivery service for spare parts and the company recommends as an important part of its servicing a maintenance agreement scheme for each machine or system. This provides a programmed of planned and preventive maintenance to allow the continuous efficiency levels.

 


SPARE PARTS


Maintaining a basis minimum stock of spares reduces downtime lending to near continuous production and most equipment breakdowns can be rectified in a mater of hours rather than days.

 


UPGRADE


Planning to upgrade you process plant – we offer cost effective solutions with minimum impact on you operation. Upgrades can be applied for:

  • Bagging Outputs
  • Upgrades to meet current industry standards.
  • Plant retrofits for different production requirements.
  • Replacing of old equipments.

 


MAINTENANCE


We offer various maintenance programmes for plants. These programmes are tailored to your needs and production levels. Contact us for further information on the various maintenance programmes and other complementary plant service programmes.


SERVICE

  • Regular maintenance and basis spares stock helps reduce possible downtime due to unforeseen emergency repairs. We have an extensive network of service engineers who have years of experience and know how in optimizing efficiencies.
