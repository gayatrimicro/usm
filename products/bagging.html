G - Series WEIGHING AND BAGGING SYSTEM

The gross bagging weighers are suitable for the accurate bagging of a large range of materials in open mouth bags. Various feeding facilities and a wide range of accessories enable the automatic gross bagging weighers to handle materials with widely differing characteristics. These features guarantee an optimum bagging procedure with high accuracy and reliability. The line is compatible with automatic bag placers and bag closing devices. The material range able to be bagged covers free flowing materials as well as difficult fluidized powders.

Standard Design

Gross weigher suitable to fill all kinds of open mouth bags. Full load cell system with exchangeable bag spouts. Pneumatic bag clamp S18. Matching feeders with full / dribble feed and cut-off gate. Electronic controller with weight display and operator prompts. Zero and secondary flow control. Over / under check. Continuous counter. Preset counter.

OPERATION

At the beginning of the weighment an empty bag is gripped on the bag spout by the bag clamp. The closing of the clamp initiates the weighment. The material is fed by the feeder in full and dribble flow to reach the accurate filling weight in the placed bag. As soon as the preset weight is achieved the material flow is cut off, the bag clamp opens automatically. The filled bag can be conveyed and the next bag can be place.

Compatibility

For placing the empty bag we recommend our automatic bag placer. We offer various bag closing devices as well as bag conveying automatic palletizing and shrink wrapping systems.
